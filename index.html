OUSD students on verge of strike over COVID-19 measures

This story is about the ongoing strike led by students at Oakland Unified School District demanding greater safety measures. Oakland teachers have been holding sick-outs throughout January to call attention to the rise in COVID cases in schools and to support the student’s call for safer schools.

Loss of Dewey bus passes spurs Oakland school district to revamp system

Last Spring, Dewey Academy, an alternative education school in Oakland Unified School District, was denied funding for their youth bus pass program. Teachers, community members, and students attended the May school board meeting to demonstrate to school board members the importance of these passes in ensuring that students get to school safely.
